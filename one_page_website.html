About Fiore Photography PDX

Photographer: Bianca McKenzie

Bianca is a native Portlander and a freelance photographer specializing in Travel, Urban and Landscape photography. I like to keep things interesting.
I've done photography recreationally for about 15 years, starting first on 35mm film in my high school photography class. Now I’ve moved on from film into the digital world but I still enjoy incorporating the old school photo techniques in the new digital environment.
This is the first time I've had the opportunity to share and publish any of my work for ya'll to enjoy. The images in my collections have been taken primarily during my 2009 study abroad trip in Spain, as well as my European adventures between 2011 and 2014. I’ve also included some more recent travel images from 2016 to 2019!
For the last couple months I've been working hard to get these images ready to share with the world! I currently have 3 images hanging up at Railhouse Lounge, for display and purchase!
All images in my gallery are available for purchase, which you can inquire further by submiting an Art Inquiry Request!
Thanks,
-Fiore (aka Bianca McKenzie)
